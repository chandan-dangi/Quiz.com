World War II Quiz Questions and Answers

Q.no 1. Which country did Germany invade first to start World War II?

Poland

Q.no 2. What was the first city to be attacked with an Atomic Bomb?

Hiroshima

Q.no 3. Who was Prime Minister when Britain declared war on Germany?

Neville Chamberlain

Q.no 4. Who was the leader of Russia during the Second World War?

Joseph Stalin

Q.no 5. How did Adolf Hitler die at the end of the war?

Suicide

Q.no 6. What was the codename for the D-Day invasion in 1944?

Operation Overlord

Q.no 7. What was the name of the battles in the Pacific?

Battle of Okinawa

Q.no 8. What country did Japan invade in 1938?

China

Q.no 9. Which army invaded the city of Berlin first?

Soviet Army

Q.no 10. What was the name of the Jewish girl whose diaries, recording her life in hiding, were published after the war?

Anne Frank

Q.no 11. What is U-Boat?

German Submarine

Q.no 12. What two countries declared war on Germany when Germany invaded Poland?

Britain and France

Q.no 13. Which technology helped Great Britain defeat the Germans in the Battle of Britain?

Radar

Q.no 14. Which fortuitous circumstance helped reduce U.S. losses at Pearl Harbor?

No American aircraft carriers were in port

Q.no 15. What area did German troops occupy in 1936?

The Rhineland

Q.no 16. Where did Germany suffer its greatest military defeats?

The Eastern Front

Q.no 17. William Hitler, Adolf Hitler's nephew, served in the Navy for which nation?

USA

Q.no 18. What was the strategy used by the US for attacking Japan?

Island hopping

Q.no 19. What was a key to the American success at the Battle of Midway?

Breaking the Code

Q.no 20. What were the Japanese fighter planes called?

Zeroes

Q.no 21. Which country lost the most civilians in WW2?

Soviet Union

Q.no 22. Which of these is a fighter plane used by the British Air Force during World War II?

Hurricane, Spitfire, and Bf 109

Q.no 23. Which place was the site of the last major battle of World War II?

Okinawa

Q.no 24. What was the name of the bomber to drop the atomic bomb on Hiroshima?

Enola Gay

Q.no 25. Operation Fork was the British invasion of what country?

Iceland

Q.no 26. Referring to a desperate maneuver used to sink Allied ships, kamikaze literally means what?

Divine wind

Q.no 27. Which occupied country managed to save over 93% of its Jewish population from capture by the Nazis?

Denmark

Q.no 28. How many US soldiers were executed for desertion?

Only one

Q.no 29. World War II: Which language was used by the US military to encode messages?

Navajo

Q.no 30. The German military operation code-named Walküre (“Valkyrie”) hoped to assassinate which leader?

Adolf Hitler
