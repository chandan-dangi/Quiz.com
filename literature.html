Literature Quiz Questions and Answers

Q.no 1. Which novel is Jacob Marley and Ebenezer Scrooge a character of?

A Christmas Carol

Q.no 2. Which Russian author published the novel War and Peace in 1867?

Leo Tolstoy

Q.no 3. Who wrote Wuthering Heights first?

Emily Bronte

Q.no 4. What was the original title of Treasure Island by Robert Louis Stevenson?

The Sea-Cook

Q.no 5. In which novel does the character Jean Valjean appear?

Les Miserables

Q.no 6. Which Dickens novel features the character Pip and his encounters with the convict Magwitch?

Great Expectations

Q.no 7. Which classic novel tells the story of Edmond Dantes seeking revenge against those who wronged him?

The Count of Monte Cristo

Q.no 8. What is the theme of the passage The Picture of Dorian Gray?

The Supremacy of Youth and Beauty

Q.no 9. Who is the author of The Strange Case of Jekyll and Hyde?

Robert Louis Stevenson

Q.no 10. Who wrote the 1897 book Dracula?

Bram Stoker

Q.no 11. Who is the author of "Little Women"?

Louisa May Alcott

Q.no 12. Which Shakespeare play features the character Iago?

Othello

Q.no 13. Who is the manipulator in Othello?

Iago

Q.no 14. Who is a Venetian faithful wife of Othello?

Desdemona

Q.no 15. What is the military rank of Othello?

General

Q.no 16. In "Heart of Darkness," what river symbolizes the journey into the dark heart of human nature and colonial exploitation?

The Congo River

Q.no 17. Who is the protagonist in Heart of Darkness?

Charles Marlow

Q.no 18. Who is the ivory trader in Heart of Darkness?

Mr. Kurtz

Q.no 19. Who wrote the novel "The Scarlet Letter"?

Nathaniel Hawthorne

Q.no 20. Which character wears the letter A in The Scarlet Letter?

Hester Prynne

Q.no 21. What is the embroidered symbol that Hester Prynne is required to wear as punishment?

The Letter 'A'

Q.no 22. Who is the father of Hester Prynne's child?

Reverend Arthur Dimmesdale

Q.no 23. Who is Hester's estranged husband who seeks revenge on her lover?

Roger Chillingworth

Q.no 24. In which novel would you find the character Scout Finch and her father, Atticus Finch?

To Kill a Mockingbird

Q.no 25. Which Romantic poet penned the famous lines "I wandered lonely as a cloud"?

William Wordsworth

Q.no 26. In which poem do the opening lines read, "Because I could not stop for Death -/ He kindly stopped for me"?

"Because I could not stop for Death"

Q.no 27. Who wrote the poem "Because I could not stop for Death"?

Emily Dickinson

Q.no 28. What epic poem explores the fall of man and the rebellion of Satan?

Paradise Lost

Q.no 29. Who wrote Paradise Lost, an epic poem?

John Milton

Q.no 30. What is the main theme of the poem "The Wasteland" by T.S. Eliot?

Brokenness and loss
