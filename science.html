Science Quiz Questions and Answers

Q.no 1. What can most insects see that humans can't?

Ultraviolet (UV) light

Q.no 2. Which cells are part of the immune system and help to fight infection?

White blood cells

Q.no 3. What is a substance made up of only one type of atom called?

Element

Q.no 4. Who first proposed the theory of relativity?

Albert Einstein

Q.no 5. What is the branch of physics that deals with the behavior of light?

Optics

Q.no 6. Which noble gas is most abundant in the Earth's atmosphere?

Argon

Q.no 7. Approximately what percent of an iceberg shows above water?

10%

Q.no 8. What is the process of a seed becoming a plant?

Germination

Electron

Q.no 10. Which hormone is responsible for love?

Oxytocin

Q.no 12. Which amount of energy raises one gram of water by one degree Celsius?

Sodium Chloride

Q.no 14. How much percent of our body is made of water?

60%

Q.no 15. What is the term for the energy an object has due to its motion?

Kinetic energy

Q.no 16. Which element has the symbol of Pb?

Lead

Q.no 17. What is the steady speed achieved by an object freely falling through a gas or liquid?

Terminal velocity

Q.no 18. What is the amount of matter present in an object?

Mass

Pyrite

Core

Mercury and Bromine

Frequency

Adenine

Q.no 25. Which type of current is produced by a battery?

Direct current (DC)

Q.no 26. What gas is used for soda?

Carbon dioxide

Q.no 27. The Richter scale is used to measure the strength of what natural event?

Earthquakes

Q.no 28. What is the primary function of carbohydrates in the human body?

Provide energy

Q.no 29. What is the term for the resistance of a liquid to flow?

Viscosity

Q.no 30. What is the most common color of obsidian?

Jet-black
